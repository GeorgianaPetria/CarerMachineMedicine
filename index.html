At-home care for only £499/week

ShareCare provides affordable care at the safety standards of live-in care.
A major driver for the enormous cost of live-in care is that it requires one carer to take care of one customer. However, a live-in carer has up to 60% dead time during a working day.
ShareCare allows two families in the same neighbourhood to share a carer, but provides 24h remote monitoring in order to prevent accidents or adverse events. By leveraging technology, our carers can leave the premises during the "dead time", but will get notified when your loved one requires help and will be on hand to assist.
Costs are lowered considerably, the quality of care is better than even and the safety is at its highest.

Best care at home in three steps



  • 1. Make a care request

    Reach out to us and we’ll respond as soon as possible. We will answer any questions you may have and start searching for a carer that suits your needs.

  • 2. Care support

    Choose your prefered carer. Our carers help with Activities of Daily Living (ADLs) like dressing, bathing, meal prep, and medication reminders to keep older adults safe at home.

  • 3. Proactive monitoring

    We use technology that monitors the house and alerts the carer when your loved one requires help. Our technology knows when a person wakes up, wanders, and it can also detect dangerous behaviours, lack of activity or falls. Because we want your loved ones never to feel the carer is not in the house, our system allows two ways communication, so that help can be requested at any time.
