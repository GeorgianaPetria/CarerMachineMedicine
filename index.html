At-home care for only £499/week

ShareCare provides affordable care at the safety standards of live-in care.
A major driver for the enormous cost of live-in care is that it requires one carer to take care of one customer. However, a live-in carer has up to 60% down time during a working day.
ShareCare allows two people living in the same neighbourhood to share a carer, but provides 24h remote video monitoring in order to prevent, or react immediately to, accidents or adverse events such as falls. By leveraging technology, our carers can leave the premises during the "down time", but will get notified when your loved one requires help and will be on hand to assist in minutes.
Costs are lowered considerably, but without compromising on the quality of care and all the while keeping safety paramount.

Best care at home in three steps



  • 1. Make a care request

    Reach out to us and we’ll respond as soon as possible. We will answer any questions you may have and start searching for a carer that suits your needs.

  • 2. Care support

    Choose your prefered carer. Our carers help with Activities of Daily Living (ADLs) like dressing, bathing, meal prep, and medication reminders to keep older adults safe at home.

  • 3. Proactive monitoring

    We use technology that monitors the house and alerts the carer if and when your loved one requires help. Our technology knows when a person wakes up, wanders, and it can also detect dangerous behaviours, lack of activity or falls. Because we want your loved one to always have the carer close at hand, our system allows two ways communication, so that help can be summoned at any time.
